Basic Guidelines (Beginner Friendly)

For BMI < 18.5:

  • Diet to include -- Milk products, Rice, Nuts, Complex Carbohydrates (e.g., Potatoes), Protein supplements
  • Exercise should include Normal weight training like -- pull ups, push ups, dips, chin ups
  • Should not perform Cardio

For 18.5 < BMI > 25:

  • Free to consume anything
  • Exercise should include intense weight training and strength straining like -- crunches, push ups, heel tap, russian twists, bicycle crunches, pull ups, dips, chin ups
  • Avoid excess of anything

For BMI > 25:

  • Diet to include -- Complex carbohydrates, low glycemic foods and fiber-rich diets, increased consumption of fruits and vegetables, legumes, whole grains, and nuts.
  • Exercise should include lots of cardio and some weight training like -- crunches, push ups, heel tap, russian twists, bicycle crunches
  • Limit fat intake and shift from saturated to unsaturated fats and avoid trans-fatty rich foods (vanaspati, bakery products, and sweets)
